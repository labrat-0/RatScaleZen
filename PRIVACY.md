# Privacy Policy for RatScaleZen

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how RatScaleZen ("we", "our", or "the extension") handles your data. RatScaleZen is an image processing tool designed to create multiple icon sizes for various platforms, all while respecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**RatScaleZen does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

All processing occurs locally on your device:
- Images are processed entirely in your browser
- Generated icon sets remain on your device
- No data is sent to external servers
- No analytics or tracking code is implemented

## Local Storage

RatScaleZen may store the following information locally on your device:
- User preferences (selected platforms)
- Window state information for proper functionality
- Temporary image data during processing

This information never leaves your device and is solely used to improve your experience with the extension.

## Permissions

RatScaleZen requires the following permissions:
- **Storage**: To save your preferences locally
- **Downloads**: To save the generated icon sets to your device

## Third-Party Services

RatScaleZen does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs. All functionality is self-contained within the extension.
